# Timeline

The canonical ALAMAT timeline is stored in `timeline_entries` and `historical_events`.

Current eras:

- Age of Origins
- Age of Spirits
- Age of Heroes
- Age of Kingdoms
- Colonial Era
- Modern Era
- The Awakening
- Current Story
- Future Timeline

Each event defines cause, effect, participating factions, participating Nilalang, and lasting consequences. These fields give quest, world, and cutscene designers a shared narrative reference without treating ALAMAT as a replacement for real history.
