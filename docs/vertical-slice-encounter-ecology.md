### Lakeshore Community

- Ka Amihan Luntian (`NPC000601`) watches shared-water boundaries and anchors the main lakeshore investigation.
- Aling Sela Dahon (`NPC000602`) teaches restrained medicinal harvesting and offers the reed-restoration side quest.
- Mang Isko Bangkero (`NPC000603`) maintains the jetty and establishes future river and coastal travel without opening unfinished regions.

All three use dawn, day, dusk, and night schedules while remaining available so time never blocks required progression.

## Reserved For Future Regions

- Siyokoy (`MON0013`) belongs in a coastal fishing village, mangrove coast, coral reef, or open-sea region.
- Magindara (`MON0017`) belongs in a reef, open-sea, storm-coast, or coastal mangrove region.
- Their codex data and prompts may remain in the repository, but neither appears in the current inland encounter pools.

## Encounter Progression

- Level 1 introduces small forest Nilalang and readable combat roles.
- Level 2 adds stronger guardians, controllers, and shrine encounters.
- Level 3 unlocks the full freshwater lakeshore ecology.
- Map pools filter by `minEncounterLevel`, preventing habitat-correct but progression-inappropriate encounters.
