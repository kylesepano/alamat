# Equipment Balance Rules

Phase F does not implement combat math, but the seed data follows these early balancing rules:

- Common and Uncommon equipment should be useful but modest.
- Rare and Epic equipment can define a build direction.
- Legendary artifacts can combine multiple bonuses, but they should remain scarce and review-heavy.
- Weapons primarily grant attack or skill hooks.
- Armor and shields primarily grant defense.
- Instruments primarily grant magic or support hooks.
- Talismans and relics primarily grant faith, spirit defense, passive hooks, or status-effect hooks.
- Monster gear should be non-extractive and focused on bonded Nilalang comfort, trust, survivability, or role support.

Future combat tuning should happen in Phase D formula data and not by rewriting the equipment schema.
