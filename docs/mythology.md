# Mythology

The mythology bible is fictional to ALAMAT.

It supports:

- creation myths
- spirit realms
- sacred places
- Nilalang origins
- human settlements
- balance of nature
- ancestral traditions
- dream realm
- underworld
- sky realm
- sea realm

Rules:

- No single tradition is the only truth.
- Do not portray one religion or culture as superior.
- Keep ALAMAT inspired by Philippine culture, not a claim about real-world belief.
- Use mythology entries as design constraints for quests, locations, Nilalang behavior, art, and endings.
