ALAMAT Gameplay Milestones

Status checkpoint after full vertical-slice playthrough QA.

Milestone 0: Production blueprint and vertical-slice roster - Complete
Milestone 1: Core engine, movement, camera, collision, interactions, save/load - Complete
Milestone 2: Combat and Nilalang systems - Playable
Milestone 3: Inventory, equipment, crafting, shops, and economy - Playable
Milestone 4: World maps, NPCs, quests, and encounter flow - Playable
Milestone 5: Story integration, companion bonding, Balon recovery, and UI polish - Playable
Milestone 6: Content expansion, balancing, final asset production, and production hardening - Next major milestone

Current Playable Slice

- Character creation supports a customizable protagonist.
- Player begins without a Nilalang companion.
- Barangay San Isidro, Balete Forest, and Spirit Shrine are playable.
- NPC interaction, quest tracking, item pickup, shops, crafting, save/load, and Balon recovery work.
- Random encounters are enabled outside safe zones.
- Batibat boss encounter gates the first companion bond.
- Aghoy can be bonded after trust and story requirements are met.
- Battle supports player and companion turns.
- Companion HP, companion XP, companion skill unlocks, and Balon revival are active.
- Player and companion battle skills are capped at six active slots.
- Skills, inventory, equipment, companion, quest, story, and progression panels are available.

Known Limitations

- Skill loadouts are functional but still use simple active/reserve toggles.
- Companion equipment exists for the vertical slice but needs a broader itemization pass.
- Maps are blockout/playtest maps, not final art maps.
- Asset replacement should continue through the Phase L asset pipeline.
- Balance values are first-pass and should be tuned after more combat QA.
- Save data is localStorage-based for the prototype.

Recommended Next Work

1. Generate and approve final PNG tilesets, item icons, equipment icons, and map previews.
2. Start Milestone 6 content expansion once core systems remain stable after another QA pass.

Completed Stabilization

- Skill loadout editing supports active and reserve states without auto-refilling intentional reserves.
- Player and companion active skill lists are capped at six slots.
- Loadout controls prevent removing the final active skill.
- Loadout controls disable activation when the six-slot limit is full.
- Player equipment and companion equipment have separate equip paths.
- Player and companion equipment can be unequipped.
- Companion gear stat totals are visible in the Companion panel.
- Companion-specific equipment can be awarded by quests.
- Companion-specific equipment can be crafted from forest and shrine materials.
- Combat balance testing now has a repeatable player-plus-companion simulator.
- Current combat balance baseline is documented in `docs/combat-balance-baseline.md`.
- Actor blockout sprites are replaced by PNG runtime spritesheets where PNG files exist.
- Map rendering supports tileset art backgrounds for approved tileset assets.
- Asset replacement audit is available with `npm run assets:audit`.
